Enjoy yourself at the Wave Pool! Play in the waves, lounge on water mats, soak in the warm water of the swirl pool, ride the thrilling 160 ft. twisting water slide or simply relax in our on-deck sauna!

All this at the only indoor wave pool within the Central Ottawa Area - a year round sun and surf destination. Plan a visit while you're in the area. It's an attraction not to be missed!

There's lots of exciting water play for all ages and it's safe. You don't have to worry about the undertow here. Our friendly, trained lifeguards make sure everyone is playing safe so you can all have fun. As well, lifejackets are available free of charge.

We have male, female and family changerooms to make your entire visit to the Wave Pool fun and convenient.
